About earning points

Learn about how to earn points!

Earning points is the number one requirement to move onto different levels in your job classes. But the only way to get to the next level in your Job Class is to earn points. There are various ways on how to get points, the main ways to get points are behavior, leadership, attendance, and work! If the people with the Repository Job think that you hav at least a %95 accuracy on all of those, you can get a point! The amount of points you are allowed a week is 7! Then you can use those points you have earned to get a new class! But that's not the only thing that points are used for... you can buy different profile pictures, some day off cards and surprise cards, even various different cards you can get from your people with the repository job... and the cards result in a positive effect and support for you! There are many different ways on getting points, and on how to spend them! Good luck on getting points, they will be the best thing for you if you are looking for a break in coding, or any various reasons at TeamsGrowth meetings or at the worldwide building TeamsGrowth Co.!
