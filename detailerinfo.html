About The Detailer Job!

The inspect on the Detailer Job.

The Detailer Job is mainly used in it's part to take control over the details in applications and help other teammates with group project for detailing. Like CSS, or styles, or decorating for TeamsGrowth events. The income of this job is $35 every week, and this job costs $45. So if you are interested in taking this job, go to the "Home" page, then scroll down to the "New beginning" card, then, select the card and find the "Detailer" Job, and then we will notify if you got the job!
