About The Informer Job!

The inspect on the Informer Job.

Everyone could sometimes forget stuff. Like important work or meetings. Which is what the Informer Job is used for! Just in case someone on the team forgets something like a meeting, team project reasearch, or even doing their OWN job... the informer job is here to inform people and help others out! From calendar problems, to even dates and notes. If someone was absent for a session at TeamsGrowth Co., then the people who have the Informer Job... can give the absent person a short and brief summary of what they did on the day the person was absent on. Thats not the only thing that the Informer Job can do, the people who has the Informer Job can help people with a bit of coding too! (Just not too much because that will overlap the responsibility of the Detailer, Styler, and sometimes the Repository job) The Informer can also be viewing everyones work, and then on "Informer Days" (Informer days will normally be held on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays), on Informer Days, the people who have the Informer Job has 1 day between every "Informer Day" to record details and data about people who were working hard, paying attention, and woud look like they would get a lot of points for their participation, behavior, track, and the working points. The income of this job is $25 on regular days, and $35 on "Informer Days" if the data sheet for viewing everyone and seeing if they are doing good or bad is fully on the paper and pencil. If not completed, the person will instead get $30 for having the paper or at least have some work on it. If the paper is lost, or there is no work on the paper, then the person who has the Informer Job will still get the $25, and this job costs $40.
