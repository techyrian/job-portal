About Job Classing

Learn about Job Classing

Job classing is like a hierachy for levels and different types of rewards you get from working hard for more levels, which will soon result into different tyoes of various fun! For example, if you just got a job at TeamsGrowth Co., or you got a job at our website TeamsGrowth, if you checked the TeamsGrowth website, you can go to the Job Classes card and see your Job Class, if it increased, or stayed the same, or even decreased! The way to move onto to different classes is by earning points (learn more on how to earn points by clicking the button below!), and once you have enough points as it is said to get to the next level, you can go on to the the next level! The more levels you earn, the more prizes and rewards and surprises you can get.
