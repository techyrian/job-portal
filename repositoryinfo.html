About The Repository Job!

The inspect on the Repository Job.

As you may have saw on the "Repository Job" button, that this is the main job of TeamsGrowth Co. This job allows you to be the lead of a team, and besides that, there are endless opportunities of being the lead of the team, some of the most important ones are to have the ability to help others, but this time, the choices lies in your hands, whether you want to help or not, it is your choice if you really feel like it. You may also choose to not do coding, because then by the group founders (other workers that take care and store data of your group, aka. attendance, track points, working points, participation points, and behavior points) none of which was listed will be marked to your choice (except the behavior points and attendance), so for the track points, working points, and participation points, will instead be marked "Repository Job Choice" if the user of the "Repository Job" decides to not do the work. The only downside thst comes from this job is having a pass for this, instead of doing it freely. When you get this job, you are handed a laminated paper, which lists a schedule for how many days you choose to ignore the points (except for behavior points, and the attendance), if you choose to skip the 3 requirements Monday-Friday, you won't be able to skip the next Monday-Friday, (more of this information will be listed on the card on the first day of work), everyone will get a printed paper which is a copy of wha the card will look like before the 2nd day, which is when the jobs are introduced and given to the people for which job they chose. The income of this job is $45 every week, and costs $60. The only downside though is that everyday skipped for the points, (except for the behavior points and the attendance), will be a 1% cut from the income, if you skipped Monday-Friday, then your income will be reduced to $40 (this reduce will be temporary, and this will be the same income for that same week, more information will be lised on the card).
